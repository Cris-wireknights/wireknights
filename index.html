About our team:

Team 19095, Wire Knight, is an FTC team based in Arad, Romania, proudly representing Colegiul National Vasile Goldis. Established in 2018, our team embarked on our journey as rookies, bringing together a passion for robotics and a commitment to innovation. Since our debut, we have strived to learn, grow, and excel in the world of FIRST Tech Challenge, building not only robots but also valuable skills in teamwork, problem-solving, and perseverance.

First Tech Challenge: Into The Deep

The 2024-2025 FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) season, "Into The Deep," invites teams to dive into a challenging underwater-themed game that emphasizes exploration, innovation, and teamwork. Teams will design and build robots to navigate obstacles, score points, and perform a variety of tasks within an aquatic environment, all while testing their engineering and programming skills. "Into the Deep" encourages creativity as teams solve unique challenges while considering aspects of mobility, precision, and automation. As the season unfolds, teams will need to adapt to new challenges, sharpen their problem-solving abilities, and demonstrate their passion for STEM. Team 19095, Wire Knight, is ready to explore the depths of competition and take on this year's challenges with determination and skill!
